About YI-KEA

Who We Are ?

YI-KEA is an autenthic furniture store with retail outlet & online store. YI-KEA was created in 2021 July 11. Since then, the company has been growing everyday.

We offer authentic of furniture related products in the market online such as Sofas, Chairs, Stools & Benches, Tables & Desks, Bookcases & Shelving Units, TV & Media Furniture, Bed Frames etc.
